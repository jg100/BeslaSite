Description

The Besla Adventurer is a high performance all terrain vehicle, designed to be usde in all sorts of locations, road, off-road, on-water, under-water, in-air, underground, and in outer space. This car can really go anywhere. With 3 sets of wheels to make sure you get where you need to go, and also because it just seemed like a cool thing to put on a car. The Adventurer ensures fun for the whole family when on camping trips to the bottom of the ocean.
